N people won the lottery and received $100 each. They made purchases and kept a spending list.
The person with the longest consecutive spending streak must pay a tax of A dollars to each person, where A is the length of the streak.
Find the difference between the person with the most and the least money after taxes.
(1 ≤ N ≤ 100, 1 ≤ Q ≤ 1000)
